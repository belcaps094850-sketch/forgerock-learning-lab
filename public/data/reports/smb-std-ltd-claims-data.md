# STD/LTD Claims Volume & Trends for SMB Markets
**Research Date:** March 5, 2026  
**Researcher:** Alec  
**Requested by:** Bel

---

## Executive Summary

Approximately **616,000 STD claims** and **75,000 LTD claims** are filed annually through employer-sponsored group disability programs in the U.S. For a typical SMB with 50 employees, that translates to roughly **1 STD claim per year** and **1 LTD claim every 13 years**. While individual claim frequency is low, the financial impact per claim is significant — averaging **$6,148 per STD claim** and **$36,484 per LTD claim**.

| **LTD conversion rate** | 5.3% of STD claims progress to LTD | — |
| **Return-to-work rate** | — | 33.5% |

*Source: IBI Benchmarking (2023 data), DMEC 2023*

## 2. What This Means for a Typical SMB

### Claims Per Year by Company Size

| Company Size | Expected STD Claims/Year | Expected LTD Claims/Year | Annual STD Cost | Annual LTD Cost |
|-------------|-------------------------|-------------------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **10 employees** | ~0.2 (1 every 5 years) | ~0.02 (1 every 65 years) | ~$1,230 | ~$560 |
| **25 employees** | ~0.4 (1 every 2.5 years) | ~0.04 (1 every 26 years) | ~$3,070 | ~$1,400 |
| **50 employees** | ~0.9 (roughly 1/year) | ~0.08 (1 every 13 years) | ~$5,530 | ~$2,800 |
| **100 employees** | ~1.8 (nearly 2/year) | ~0.15 (1 every 6.5 years) | ~$11,070 | ~$5,600 |

*Calculated using: STD incidence 1.76/100, LTD incidence 1.54/1,000, avg payments per claim*

### Key Insight for SMBs
- An individual SMB won't process many claims, but **each claim has outsized impact** on a small team
- A single employee on STD for 69 days (average) in a 10-person company = 10% of workforce gone
- SMBs are the **least equipped** to handle the admin burden of even 1 claim per year

## 4. Trends (2021 → 2023)

| Trend | Direction | Detail |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| STD incidence rate | ↓ 27% | Dropped from 2.41 to 1.76 per 100 (post-COVID normalization) |
| LTD incidence rate | ↓ 21% | Dropped from 1.91 to 1.54 per 1,000 |
| STD cost per claim | ↑ 3.1% | $5,963 → $6,148 (rising despite fewer claims) |
| LTD cost per claim | ↑ 15.8% | $31,513 → $36,484 (significant increase) |
| STD duration | ↓ 15.8% | 82.3 → 69.3 days (claims resolving faster) |
| LTD conversion rate | ↓ 40% | 14.2% → 5.3% (fewer STD claims becoming LTD) |
| Return-to-work rate | ↑ 25% | 26.8% → 33.5% (more people returning to work) |

**Key takeaway:** Fewer claims are being filed, but each claim costs more. The cost-per-claim inflation is outpacing the frequency decline.

## 5. Coverage Gap — The SMB Problem

### Access to Disability Insurance (BLS)
- **~40% of private sector workers** have access to STD
- **~35% of private sector workers** have access to LTD
- **65% of private sector workers** have NO long-term disability insurance
- Coverage rates are **significantly lower** at small firms vs. large employers

### The Numbers That Matter
- **51 million working adults** have no disability insurance beyond Social Security
- **1 in 4** of today's 20-year-olds will be disabled before retirement age
- **~5% of working Americans** experience a short-term disability each year
- Average SSDI benefit: **$1,581/month** ($18,972/year — below poverty line)
- SSDI approval rate: only **30%** of applications approved
- SSDI processing time: **3-5 months** initial, appeals backlog **231 days**

### Financial Consequences
- **77.8%** of consumer bankruptcy filers cited income loss as a contributor
- **44.3%** specifically cited medically-related work loss
- Cancer patients are **71% more likely** to experience severe adverse financial events

## 7. Implications for Solution Design

### For Claims Concierge / ClaimTrack:
- **~200K claims/year** in the SMB segment = addressable volume
- Each claim lasts **69 days (STD) to 34 months (LTD)** = long engagement window
- Average claim value ($6K STD, $36K LTD) justifies premium service fees
- Mental health claims (11% of STD, 10% of LTD) are growing and most complex to navigate

### For Benefits in a Box / Comparison Portal:
- **4-5 million SMBs** without disability coverage = massive untapped market
- **65% of private workers** lack LTD = product awareness is a barrier, not just access
- Cost per claim is rising (+3% STD, +16% LTD) = employers need help managing costs

### For Benefits Copilot:
- Pregnancy (22% of STD) = AI can proactively guide employers on leave planning
- Musculoskeletal (17% STD, 25% LTD) = AI can flag ergonomic risks and prevention
- Mental health growing = AI can recommend EAP and wellness programs
